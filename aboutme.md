BE-SAFE is a collaborative research project within the **UNITA European University Alliance**, bringing together universities and municipalities to improve cyclist safety in modern urban environments.

### Mission

Our goal is to develop tools, methods, and policy recommendations that make cycling safer in smart cities — through data-driven analysis, infrastructure assessment, and stakeholder engagement.

### Consortium

The project unites academic partners and municipal stakeholders across Europe under the UNITA framework.

### Funding

BE-SAFE is supported by the UNITA Alliance joint research programme.
